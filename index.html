About Me

I am a PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Kansas. My research interests include battery modeling, state-space modeling, system identification, and scientific machine learning.

My current work focuses on physics-informed and data-driven methods for battery health estimation, parameter identification, and forecasting.
